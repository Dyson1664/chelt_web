Style Wednesday

The Cheltenham Festival is a horse racing-based meeting in the National Hunt racing calendar in the United Kingdom.

Style Wednesday celebrates speed, style and exhilaration. Featuring the Queen Mother Champion chase, it is fast, furious, and requires a perfect blend of speed and an ability to be inch-perfect when negotiating each of the 13 fences at pace around Cheltenham’s legendary old course. The fastest Jump horses jumping at speed with the upmost precision and accuracy. It is simply spinetingling.
